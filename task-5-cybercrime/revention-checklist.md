Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
✅ Do’s
Always verify the identity of the person before making any payment
Use only trusted and official payment apps
Enable two-factor authentication and app lock
Check transaction details before confirming payments
Monitor bank statements regularly
❌ Don’ts
Never share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Never scan QR codes to receive money
Do not accept unknown payment requests
Avoid making transactions on public Wi-Fi
💳 UPI Safety Tips
Always check the receiver’s name before sending money
Keep a daily transaction limit for safety
🚨 Report Cybercrime
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
